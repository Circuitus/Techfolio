Welcome to the page that will tell you about my thought processes in making this website


Let's start with the compass on the homepage

'Compass' has kind of become my tag for everything. For stuff I do in school, like kahoot and quizlet, and stuff I do outside of school, like as my tag in discord. One could say it has almost become an alternate persona for me, and if I can't use the English word compass, I will either go to one of the non-generic English ways of saying compass, like 'northseeker', or use the word compass in a foreign language, like how 'boussole' means compass in French.

So, when I use the compass on the homepage, it means that this website represents me. That I have made it mine and that I have personalised it. That this website is my, original, work.
